About Us

A Canadian Business Built for Global Trade

Founded in 2024, MUSAO Group is a Canadian import-export company focused on making international trade more accessible for businesses of all sizes.

We work closely with our clients to understand their specific needs, whether that means finding the right supplier, navigating customs and compliance, or getting products to customers across North America.

We are at the beginning of our journey and proud of it. Every client relationship we build is one we intend to keep for the long term, grounded in transparency, clear communication, and genuine commitment.

What We Do

Our Services

We offer a focused set of services designed to simplify cross-border trade, adaptable to your industry and the nature of your products.

🌐

Import & Export

We facilitate cross-border trade between Canada, the United States, and international markets, handling logistics coordination and compliance requirements.

🔍

Product Sourcing

We help identify and vet suppliers that match your quality and reliability standards, saving you the time and uncertainty of searching on your own.

📦

Distribution

We help streamline your supply chain to make your products more accessible to customers in Canada and the United States.

🏪

Market Access

We support international companies looking to enter the Canadian or US market with local guidance and distribution connections.

📋

Trade Compliance

We help ensure your transactions meet the relevant customs and regulatory requirements, reducing the risk of delays or complications.

💼

Business Consulting

For businesses new to international trade, we offer practical guidance to help you understand your options and plan your next steps.
